Who am I? Why I started this page?

There’s a certain kind of nervousness that creeps in when someone asks, "What’s your dream?" We’ve all felt it at some point, haven’t we? But there was a time when my eyes would light up at that question. "I want to be an engineer." That was my little dream, the one I clung to like a lighthouse in the fog.

And then, something changed. My eyes started wandering—from this to that, from certainty to confusion in a heartbeat. Indecisiveness seeped into my veins, and a weight settled on my chest, one I couldn’t shake.

That’s why this little corner of the internet came into existence. All the words and emotions racing through my mind— I won’t let them stay trapped. Like Dumbledore drawing memories from the mind, I’ll store them here.

I hope you find something here that stirs you, something that lingers. Lots of love.
