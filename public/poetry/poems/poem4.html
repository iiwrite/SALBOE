Reds and Blues

Photo by Wolfgang Hasselmann on Unsplash

I went to lands unknown To collect rubies on my own For each ruby found, I carved Into a heart, with a piece of me inside.

Adorned with roses in a box, And ribbons wrapped all around. I gave them all to you One by one, into your waiting palm.

Love glimmered in your eyes, With a subtle shade of qualm.

In the search for rubies, I found sapphires. With all the sapphires found, I forged them into armor.

I wore the armor over my heart. It kept me safe when you smiled. When my heart skipped with your kiss, It did not let its guard down, even for a while.

Yet it failed when you chose to leave. I am left with sapphire's melt as I grieve. All the red gifted, only the blue remains with me. Take the blue too, leave me colourless, set me free.
